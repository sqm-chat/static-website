This is our story.

Spending years working in the restaurant and supply industry, founders Ty Wilson and Sam Unterman saw a huge gap in the communicative and transactional technology connecting suppliers and restaurants. They spent a year building tools to close that gap. The tools play in to how things are done now, causing minimal friction and a seamless integration and immersive experience

Why we created Tab

The pandemic devastated the restaurant and supply industry. When that happened many restaurants decided to stop relying on the primary means of distribution, using large distributors like Sysco and USFoods. Instead, they started focusing on supply chains between restaurants and local suppliers. The decision, paired with the increased use of electronic payment tools paved the way for Tab to help the industry source more local supply, and provided the tools to seamlessly carry it out
